I worked on solving retail business problems using PostgreSQL queries and a structured data analysis approach.

My methodology involved understanding the business requirements, identifying key data questions related to sales, products, and locations, and then writing SQL queries to extract meaningful insights from the dataset. 

I applied data retreiving, filtering, sorting, and conditional logic techniques to analyze trends such as high-value orders, product performance, and regional sales differences.

One challenge I encountered was ensuring the queries matched the exact business requirements while maintaining accurate query syntax and proper ordering/filtering of results in PostgreSQL.
